Bamboo: Fundamental Portfolio

Our strong technical and fundamental knowledge ensures that your capital creates an alpha in all phases of the market. We strive to deliver alpha by participating, for the long term, in growth of the market leaders during a bull cycle and by avoiding exposure to the laggards during a bear cycle.

The fundamental portfolio has two major allocations:

  • Core Portfolio (50-70% allocation): Long term bets | Holding period of more than 3 years | Low turnover | Hold during price consolidation | Sell only if deteriorating fundamentals or end of primary cycle | Position sizing according to the current market primary cycle
  • Momentum Portfolio (30-50% of allocation): Technical based entry & exit | Opportunistic cash trades in fundamentally good companies | Index option buying trades (12-48 opportunities in a year)

Capital Compound Insight – Beta for the best companies/leaders of a bull run is high when market goes up, while Beta for the same companies is low when market goes down (A concept familiarly known as Relative Strength)

Capital Compound Insight – Rigorous Technical analysis hedges portfolio as well as catches potential leaders of a bull run through an independent and complementary process to fundamental analysis

Snowball: Trading Portfolio

We follow a disciplined trading strategy with sound risk management principles

Following factors reflect our approach to the trading portfolio:

  • Directional trades with average holding duration of 3-4 days to 2 weeks
  • Risk is defined per trade before entry | Disciplined risk management | All overnight short positions are hedged
  • Over 90% of trades are in index options | Opportunistic cash bets are also taken occasionally
  • Minimum capital required is INR 10 lakhs

    • Capital Compound Insight – A mandatory three point check list before taking any trade can improve your returns drastically - capital at risk (never risk more than you can afford to lose), risk-reward ratio and the stop loss.

    Capital Compound Insight – Trading after market confirmations (as opposed to anticipations) may reduce your per trade returns, but will drastically improve your success rate, helping you compound your money consistently.

Ant Portfolio: Make your money work smarter

You may not always have sizeable cash available to create your own trading portfolio from scratch but may have some assets invested in mutual funds. We can help you collateralize your existing holdings with the exchange and essentially monetize your existing portfolio without liquidating it. The proceeds would be used to take cash trades in stocks exhibiting technical strength.

  • Utilize your long term dormant investments for cash trading
  • Generate additional income/returns without bringing in more capital
  • Diversified cash trading bets with only partial portfolio deployment to generate returns with low risk

Capital Compound Insight - Understanding the difference between saving and investing is crucial for long term economic health. You work so hard to save for the future, it is probably time to make your money work smarter to help you achive your goals faster.
