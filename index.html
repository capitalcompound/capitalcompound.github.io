Snowball: Trading Portfolio

Ant: Make your money work smarter

Generate additional returns without bringing in more capital. Utilize your dormant equity investments to generate excess returns without liquidating your long-term porfolio by collateralizing them.

Our process - Our trades are exactly the same as our trading product 'Snowball', while you have the discretion to either retain your current portfolio or to align with the allocations we follow as per our product - 'Bamboo'. The difference between 'Snowball' and 'Ant' is the amount of risk you take, by accepting the dual volatility of the value of your long-term investments and our trading bets to enable you to achieve your goals faster.

Hill: Invest in a constrained environment

If you are constrained by SEBI or your employer because you may have access to price sensitive information, you have landed in the perfect place. You can now benefit from our expertise in understanding and exploiting the price behavior of Nifty 50 - an asset class never restricted by SEBI due to the indices' diversification across its constituent companies.

Our process - Our trades follow the same decision making process as 'Snowball', while the strategy is built around only Nifty 50 instruments. This ensures that you manage your investment constaints while making your money work smarter instead of parking them in FDs, Nifty ETFs and index funds.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I trust Capital Compound?

    Our experience, credibility, transparency and skin-in-the-game are the biggest reasons why everyone trusts us. We onboard only our potentials who fulfil our eligibility criteria. This ensures that the required level of trust builds and exists for a long-term relationship.

  • Is there an eligibility criteria for me to be an investor?

    Yes, please get in touch with us only if you can -

    1. Accept the risk - Ensure you have a capability to risk our minimum investment amount, which is currently ₹ 10 lacs.

    2. Ignore daily movements - Investments do not go up like FDs, so please focus on cumulative returns for at least 3+ years, and not on daily, monthly or even annual returns. Wealth creation is a long process, and we aspire to be your partners in this journey.

    3. Think long term - Look at a long-term relationship for growing wealth beyond ₹2 crores and not for withdrawing short term profits.

    4. Act professionally - We follow a well disciplined code of conduct built on Respect, Equality, Trust and Transparency - and we expect you to act in a similar manner at all times.

  • How do I pick the best investment product for my needs?

    Connect with us. Our team will discuss your current financial allocation, upcoming commitments and suggest the best suitable product to help you achieve your goals. However, it is strongly recommended to consult your independent financial advisor before taking any investment decisions.

  • Is there any lock-in on my Investments?

    There is no lock-in on your investments. But we at Capital Compound strongly recommend a time horizon of 3-5 years for the Bamboo portfolio and 1 year for the Snowball and Ant Portfolio to safeguard against near term volatility in markets and to be given a fair chance to demonstrate our skills. However, if you are in a need of liquidity, you can receive the then current value of your investment in your bank account within 1-3 working days.

  • Is there any minimum capital requirement?

    Yes, there is a minimum investment requirement of ₹ 10 lacs. Get in touch with us to receive suggestions on possible alternates of structuring this investment. Also, please note that our minimum investment will go up to ₹ 25 lacs, and we will keep updating this as our personal bandwidth reduces.

  • Does Capital Compound Guarantee returns?

    No, we do not guarantee any returns. One may plan to commit with us once you are ready to understand and accept the risks associated. Please ensure that you clear your liabilities first and do not borrow, we expect your transparency and prudence in keeping personal financial discipline.

  • How will my investments be managed?

    All your investments are held in a trading and demat account in your name with a third party broker, the control to which only you have. We simply take the required permission to execute trades, but the control on flow of funds is fully in your control. For further details, please get in touch with us.

  • Why is Capital Compound serving only through a third-party broker?

    We want to provide best-in-class service to all of you. Our partnership with a bank-backed third-party broker ensures safety of capital. Our relationship with them enables us to offer personalized attention to all along with the powerful trading system of a full-service broker. With our transaction volumes, we are also able to offer you the best brokerage rates negotiated specially for you.

  • Are there any hidden charges and exit load?

    No, we charge only the performance-based fee. Our fee structure is designed with a customer-delight approach. Unlike mutual funds and almost all PMS and AIF services, we do not charge any fixed fee. Even after all the applicable fees, each of our offerings still offer the potential to beat all other asset classes over time.

  • Can I withdraw profits intermittently?

    This is not recommended. We are only looking for investors who want to grow their capital beyond ₹2 crores in the next few years. As our personal bandwidth reduces, we will only be able to serve you with a larger commitment.

  • Derivatives are very risky. Can my account blow up?

    If tried on your own without knowledge and experience, there are chances of losing the entire capital. Hiring experts to do it for you ensures that all precautions are taken to avoid this from happening. We take these precautions (stop losses and hedges on all our trades) to ensure that all losses are minimized. Though there is no way to pre-empt a trade and take only the profitable ones, we leverage our data-backed insights minimize the probability of a trade making a loss. The conservative maximum drawdown that you may witness in Snowball is -25% at any point in time, which eventually recovers as the market normalizes or the strategy starts to perform again.
